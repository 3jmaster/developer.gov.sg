Overview

This handbook comprises suggested approach, key principles and other reference resources that will serve as a guide to Ministry Family Chief Digital Strategy Officers (CDSOs) and relevant officers involved in developing digitalisation plans for their agencies.

This version is adapted from the guide given to Chief Digital Strategy Officers for formulating their Ministry Family Digitalisation Plans.
